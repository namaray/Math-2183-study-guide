2. Linear Systems and Rank

[cite_start]

Consistency is determined by comparing the rank of the coefficient matrix ($r_A$) and the augmented matrix ($r_{Ab}$) against the number of variables ($n$) [cite: 521, 907-910].

Type of $g(x)$ Trial Form of $y_p$
Constant (e.g., 1)$A$ [cite: 158]
Linear (e.g., $5x+7$)$Ax + B$ [cite: 158]
Trig ($\sin 4x$ or $\cos 4x$)$A \cos 4x + B \sin 4x$ [cite: 158]
Exponential ($e^{5x}$)$Ae^{5x}$ [cite: 158]

3. Matrix Transformations

[cite_start]

Transformations map a vector $x$ to $w$ using $T(x) = Ax$[cite: 667, 674].

[cite_start]

Common 2D Matrices [cite: 714, 870]

    [cite_start]
  • Reflection about x-axis: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{bmatrix}$ [cite: 874]
    • [cite_start]
  • Reflection about y-axis: $\begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$ [cite: 876]
    • [cite_start]
  • Reflection about $y=x$: $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$ [cite: 879]
    • [cite_start]
  • Enlargement (Scale factor $k$): $\begin{bmatrix} k & 0 \\ 0 & k \end{bmatrix}$ [cite: 884]
    • [cite_start]
  • Rotation (Anticlockwise $\theta$): $\begin{bmatrix} \cos \theta & -\sin \theta \\ \sin \theta & \cos \theta \end{bmatrix}$ [cite: 714]
