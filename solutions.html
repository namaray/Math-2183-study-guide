Problem 13 (i)

System: $x-y+2z=3$, $3x+y-2z=0$, $-x+2y-z=-1$[cite: 51].

Step 1: Augmented Matrix
$$\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & -1 & 2 & 3 \\ 3 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 & -1 & -1 \end{array}\right]$$
Step 2: Row Operations

$R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 3R_1$ and $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + R_1$ [cite: 556, 931-932]:

$$\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & -1 & 2 & 3 \\ 0 & 4 & -8 & -9 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right]$$

Swap $R_2, R_3$, then $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 4R_2$:

$$\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & -1 & 2 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 0 & -12 & -17 \end{array}\right]$$
Step 3: Analysis

Rank(A) = 3, Rank(Ab) = 3, Unknowns(n) = 3[cite: 562, 570].

Unique Solution: x = 0.75, y = 0.58, z = 1.42
